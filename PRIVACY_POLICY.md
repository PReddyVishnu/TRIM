# Privacy Policy for TRIM

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023

TRIM ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection
TRIM is a local-first application. Most of your data is stored securely on your device. However, to provide cloud synchronization features, we collect:
*   **Subscription Data:** Name of service, cost, billing cycle, and category.
*   **Authentication Data:** If you choose to use Cloud Sync, we use Google Sign-In to authenticate your account and store your user ID.

## 2. Use of Data
We use the collected data solely to:
*   Provide and maintain the service (Cloud Sync).
*   Calculate spending analytics displayed within the app.
*   Trigger local notifications for upcoming renewals.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use **Google Firebase (Firestore & Auth)** for data storage and authentication. Your data is protected by Google's industry-standard security protocols. We do not sell your data to third parties.

## 4. Data Retention
You can delete your data at any time by:
*   Deleting individual subscriptions in the app.
*   Uninstalling the app (local data).
*   Requesting account deletion (cloud data).

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us within the app support channels.
